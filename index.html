Frequently Asked Questions

What is School Sport ME?

Football Made Easy is a high quality coaching solution in the form of a mobile app. It has been designed to enable coaches and parents to conduct great training sessions with confidence & ease.

Is School Sport Made Easy suitable for my school?

Parents of junior players = Yes. Coaches of junior teams = Yes. Junior Players = Yes.

What if I have multiple classes in different age groups?

We give you access to all Modules for all age groups, so you can switch according to who is using the App.

What is included in the App?

Hundreds of drills, games, and coaching tips you require to conduct great training or practice sessions. Follow our Session Plans or use the content how ever you like.

Who designed and makes all the content in SSME?

Kory Babington and a team of highly qualified coaches have spent almost 3 years creating the content. Watch the video above about Kory.

How can I get this for my school?

After your Free Trial you can continue for less than the cost of a cup of coffee per week. $9.99 AUD per month is our default subscription cost, however you have the option to choose an annual or weekly subscription. The most you will pay is $2.99 per week.

How does this help Primary School teachers?

Absolutely. We have great deals for clubs so contact us and we will do all the work for you.
